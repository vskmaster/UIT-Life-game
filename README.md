# 🎮 UIT Life – Real Life Simulation Game

## Overview

**UIT Life** is an immersive real-life simulation game inspired by the everyday experiences of students at **United Institute of Technology (UIT)**. The game allows players to navigate academic challenges, build friendships, participate in campus activities, manage resources, and shape their own college journey through meaningful choices and decisions.

Designed as both an entertaining and educational experience, UIT Life recreates the excitement, struggles, and opportunities of student life in a virtual environment.

## Features

✨ **Student Life Simulation**

* Experience college life from admission to graduation.
* Make decisions that influence your academic and personal growth.

📚 **Academic Progression**

* Attend classes and complete assignments.
* Improve skills and maintain academic performance.

💰 **Resource Management**

* Manage money, energy, time, and reputation.
* Balance studies, social life, and extracurricular activities.

🤝 **Social Interaction System**

* Build friendships and connections.
* Interact with classmates, faculty members, and campus communities.

🏆 **Achievements & Progression**

* Unlock achievements through milestones and accomplishments.
* Track your growth throughout your college journey.

🎯 **Mini Challenges & Events**

* Participate in competitions, workshops, hackathons, and campus events.
* Earn rewards and improve your character's abilities.

🌍 **Open Campus Exploration**

* Explore different areas of the campus.
* Discover opportunities, hidden interactions, and unique experiences.

## Gameplay

Players begin their journey as a student entering UIT and must make choices that impact their future. Every action affects various aspects of student life including:

* Academics
* Skills Development
* Social Relationships
* Financial Stability
* Career Growth
* Campus Reputation

The goal is to successfully navigate college life while building the best version of your virtual self.
